About

Michael Jackson was a highly influential American singer, songwriter, and dancer, dubbed the "King of Pop," who was active for over four decades. He began his career as a child star in the Jackson 5 before achieving massive success as a solo artist with albums like Thriller. His career was defined by revolutionary music videos, innovative dance moves like the moonwalk, and global fame, though it was also marked by later legal issues.

Work

Michael Jackson est connu pour des chansons comme Thriller, Billie Jean et Beat It. Il a révolutionné l'industrie du disque, notamment en créant des clips musicaux de grande qualité comparable à des courts-métrages et en devenant l'une des personnalités les plus célèbres du XXe siècle. Il est aussi célèbre pour des titres comme Don't Stop 'Til You Get Enough, Black or White et Smooth Criminal. Chansons populaires Thriller: (1982) Billie Jean: (1982) Beat It: (1982) Don't Stop 'Til You Get Enough: (1979) Black or White: (1991) Smooth Criminal: (1987) Rock With You: (1979) Bad: (1987) Remember the Time: (1991) Human Nature: (1982)
